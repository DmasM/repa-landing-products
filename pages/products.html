Custom Labels & Tags

Repacorp can manufacture just about any custom label or tag your customer might need. We offer multiple printing processes, adhesives, materials, inks, varnishes, multi-laminates, configurations, and core sizes to satisfy your customer’s needs.

Delivering high-quality and high speed output, our flexo presses can handle up to 12 colors in one pass with web widths up to 11.5” and 4-color process with web widths up to 20”. For short runs, our 4-color process digital presses offer the ability to print opaque white ink and black variable print with precise laser cutting. Our high-speed digital press offers 4-color process, an opaque white option, cold foil and two additional flexo stations for PMS colors, fluorescents, metallic ink or spot varnish. If you need harsh environment, asset control, or warehouse management labels, our thermal transfer press offers up to 4 ribbon colors and prints on almost any material.

RFID Labels & Tags

Due to the increase in demand for Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) enabled labels and tags, Repacorp has installed our third RFID press in our Ohio facility. This 8-color RFID press has a 13" web width and servo printing stations. The simplicity of the print station design helps to guarantee consistency and accuracy, resulting in exceptional print precision for pressure sensitive labels and non-pressure sensitive tags.

Repacorp offers stock, as well as custom solutions, for a wide range of RFID applications. We stock 4" x 1", 4" x 2" and 4" x 6" RFID labels formatted for most industry-standard printers. Repacorp manufactures custom RFID labels and tags in a variety of sizes, materials and with a wide range of inlays. All of our smart labels are 100% verified inline prior to shipment.

Wide Format Digital Printing

Repacorp’s Arizona facility can run roll-to-roll wide formats that include posters, banners, backlit signs, fine art, billboards, and decals. Rigid substrates open up markets for signs, transit advertising, exhibits and displays. We can print on substrates up to 1.9" thick and 98.4" wide with flexible UV inks that do not crack when the printed piece is bent or stretched. This press' superb detail is due to a maximum output resolution of 1,500 dpi, and incorporates drop-on-demand, eight-level grayscale printing heads. The variable ink drop technology enables smooth gradations for photo-quality color printing. It prints up to 21 layers of ink for textured images. Multiple image layering opens up possibilities to produce Braille formats for ADA graphics and double-sided window graphics. Watch a video about our Truepress Jet2500UV’s capabilities.

Repacorp’s screen presses can print 38” x 52” or 52” X 120”. Whether your clients require floor graphics, counter mats, or magnetic signs, Repacorp is ready to assist you with a divers suite of specialty printing solutions.
